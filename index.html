About Me

Hello! My name is Sam Azouzi and I am an aspiring software engineer. Ever since playing around with my first computer I've been addicted to learning more about this box that has revolutionized the way we live. Coming to Rutgers I met an amazing community of computer scientists, like me, with similar passions for technology which only motivated me more. I've been to several Hackathons where I coded really cool things like a bot that read input and would respond based on the emotional sentiment of the text to a mobile application that could take a picture of a person with an expression and place the corresponding emoji on their faces as a result. I've also taken every opportunity that I could to show how much I appreciate this community, as you will see below. I am currently a Senior and not once have I thought I was in the wrong place. I continously enjoy the material I learn in my classes and am motivated to build projects around my study. I am currently very interested in solving Big Data problems in Distributed manners. This includes cloud infrastructure and Data Anaylsis. Thanks so much for reading!
