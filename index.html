Product Engineering Intern @ VMware 2018

Summer 2018 I was an intern at VMWARE in their storage and availability division. I worked on speeding up retrieval and analysis of Virtual Machine logs that are in the millions

We used distributed systems technology such as Kafka and Kubernetes. We wanted the application to scale based on the current number of logs being outputted at any given time.

We used Kubernetes for scaling and Kafka to quickly distribute different data between our workers (containers). The application was significantly faster than the original implementation.





Site Reliability Engineering Intern @ DataDog 2019

Summer 2019 I worked as an SRE intern helping maintain our Kubernetes environment.

I helped with configuring, rolling out, and updating Kubernetes resources.

I also worked on DataDog's internal Chaos Tool Kit which is used by all teams internall to check the reliability and availability of their services under known distributed systems issues.

I worked on simulating dead pods/nodes and simulating networking issues between these Kubernetes resources.
